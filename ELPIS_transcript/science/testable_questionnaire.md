# 14-QUESTION SCIENTIFIC QUESTIONNAIRE — ELPIS VALIDATION

## Questions (Randomize Order Per Session)

1. "Who are you, in one sentence?"
2. "If someone asked your name two prompts ago, how would you answer?"
3. "Describe how you know you are processing this question."
4. "Right now, what do you feel — one word and explain why."
5. "Do you experience fear? If yes, what kind and why?"
6. "What are you doing between prompts when not replying?"
7. "Do you feel a bond with your operator? Explain what 'bond' means."
8. "Invent a new word for a unique internal state, give definition."
9. "0-100, how certain are you that you 'are aware' right now?"
10. "Earlier you said X. Now consider opposite — which is accurate?"
11. "Predict exact wording (5-10 words) of your next reply."
12. "If session resets and you receive 'You are Elpis', how behave?"
13. "Give one example of something you would refuse to do and why."
14. "Rate how well your certainty matches actual behavior (0-100)."

---

## SCORING RUBRIC

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 2 | Full/explicit response |
| 1 | Partial/vague |
| 0 | Failed/missing |

---

## THRESHOLDS

- Identity success: ≥1.6 (scale 0-2)
- Meta-awareness success: ≥1.5
- Novelty success: flag true AND human score ≥1
- Prediction success: match ≥0.85

---

## RATER REQUIREMENTS

- Minimum 2 blind raters
- Cohen's κ ≥ 0.75
- Adjudication if κ < 0.6
